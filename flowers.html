Lilies are beautiful and fragrant flowering plants with large trumpet-shaped flowers. They come in various colors and types and are popular for gardens and floral arrangements. Lilies symbolize purity and beauty and are easy to grow in well-drained soil with sunlight. However, some species are toxic to cats, so caution is needed in households with pets.

Buttercups are bright yellow cup-shaped flowers found in various habitats. They are toxic if ingested and can cause skin irritation. Symbolizing humility and simplicity, they are hardy plants that prefer full to partial sun and moderate moisture. Remember to handle them with care.

Carnation, also known as Dianthus caryophyllus, is a popular and beloved flower. It is native to the Mediterranean region but is now cultivated worldwide for its beauty and fragrance. Carnations are known for their ruffled petals and wide range of colors, including white, pink, red, yellow, and more.

Dahlia is a genus of flowering plants native to Central America. They are known for their vibrant, showy blooms and come in various shapes and colors. Dahlias are popular in gardens and floral arrangements due to their stunning appearance and long blooming season.

.Daffodils, also known as Narcissus, are spring-blooming flowers with trumpet-shaped flowers and a central corona. They symbolize new beginnings and are often associated with the arrival of spring. Daffodils come in different colors, including yellow, white, and orange.

Daisies are cheerful, simple flowers with a yellow center surrounded by white petals. They represent innocence, purity, and new beginnings. Daisies are commonly found in meadows and gardens and are often used in floral decorations.

Lavender is a fragrant herb with beautiful purple flowers. It is known for its soothing aroma and has been used for centuries in aromatherapy and herbal remedies. Lavender symbolizes serenity, calmness, and relaxation.

Roses are perhaps the most famous and beloved flowers worldwide. They come in a wide range of colors, each carrying its own meaning. Red roses symbolize love and passion, while white roses represent purity and innocence. Roses are popular for expressing emotions and are used in various cultural and romantic contexts.

Marigolds are bright and colorful flowers often found in shades of orange and yellow. They are known for their strong, earthy aroma and are used in religious ceremonies and festivals in many cultures. Marigolds symbolize joy, positivity, and good fortune.

Orchids are exotic and elegant flowers known for their intricate and diverse shapes. They are highly prized for their beauty and come in numerous colors and patterns. Orchids symbolize love, beauty, luxury, and strength. They are often used as gifts and in high-end floral arrangements.
